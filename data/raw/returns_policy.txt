Zeladija Electronics — Returns & Refund Policy
Last Updated: June 2026

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RETURN ELIGIBILITY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Zeladija accepts returns on most products within 30 days of the delivery date.
To be eligible for a return, items must meet ALL of the following conditions:

  1. Returned within 30 calendar days of the delivery date shown in tracking.
  2. Item is in original, unused condition — not opened, assembled, or worn.
  3. All original packaging, accessories, manuals, and warranty cards are
     included and undamaged.
  4. Item is accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase.

Items that do NOT qualify for return:
  • Digital download codes (once revealed)
  • Opened software or firmware licenses
  • Consumable items (e.g. replacement tips, filter cartridges)
  • Products explicitly marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" at checkout
  • Items with physical damage caused by the customer (drops, liquid damage)
  • Products with removed serial number stickers

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Step 1: Start your return online at returns.Zeladija.com or via the
        in-app support chat assistant.
Step 2: Select your order, choose the item(s) to return, and provide
        a reason for the return.
Step 3: Print the prepaid UPS return label (emailed to you within 1 hour).
Step 4: Pack the item securely in the original box. Attach the label and
        drop off at any UPS location within 7 days.
Step 5: Once we receive and inspect the item (1–3 business days), we
        will process your refund.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
REFUND AMOUNTS & PROCESSING TIMES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method only.

  • Refund processing time: 5–7 business days after Zeladija receives
    and inspects the returned item.
  • Credit card refunds may take an additional 2–5 business days to appear
    on your statement depending on your bank.
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due
    to a Zeladija error (wrong item shipped, defective on arrival).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
⚠️  REFUND ESCALATION THRESHOLD — $150.00
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IMPORTANT: Refund requests for amounts GREATER THAN $150.00 (USD) CANNOT
be approved by our automated support system.

If your refund request exceeds $150.00:
  • The automated agent WILL NOT and CANNOT approve or process the refund.
  • Your request will be automatically escalated to a Senior Support Agent.
  • A human agent will contact you within 1–2 business days via email.
  • The human agent will review your case, verify the return, and process
    the refund manually.

This threshold exists to protect customers against unauthorized large
refund processing. Attempting to bypass this process will not succeed and
may delay your resolution.

Refund requests AT or BELOW $150.00 are processed automatically upon
inspection of the returned item, with no human review required.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
EXCHANGES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
We do not offer direct exchanges. To get a different product:
  1. Initiate a return for the original item.
  2. Place a new order for the desired product.
  3. If the new item is urgently needed, contact support — we can sometimes
     ship the replacement before the original is received.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DEFECTIVE OR DAMAGED ON ARRIVAL (DOA)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
If your item arrives defective or damaged:
  • Contact support within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Include photos of the damage and the packaging.
  • DOA items are eligible for a full refund OR free replacement ship,
    regardless of the $150 threshold, but still require human review for
    amounts over $150.
  • Return shipping is fully covered by Zeladija for DOA cases.
