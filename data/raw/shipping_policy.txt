Zeladija Electronics — Shipping Policy
Last Updated: June 2026

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
OVERVIEW
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Zeladija is committed to fast, reliable delivery of your electronics. We ship
to all 50 US states, US territories, and over 80 international destinations.
All orders are subject to the terms below.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DOMESTIC SHIPPING OPTIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Standard Shipping (5–7 Business Days)
  Cost: FREE on orders over $50 | $5.99 for orders under $50
  Details: The most economical option for non-urgent orders. Delivered via
  UPS Ground or USPS Priority Mail depending on destination zip code. A
  tracking number is emailed once the package is picked up by the carrier.

Express Shipping (2–3 Business Days)
  Cost: $12.99 flat rate
  Details: Delivered via UPS Second Day Air or equivalent. Orders placed
  before the daily cutoff (see below) ship the same business day.

Overnight Shipping (Next Business Day)
  Cost: $24.99 flat rate
  Details: Delivered via UPS Next Day Air. Guaranteed next-business-day
  delivery to most US addresses. Saturday delivery not included unless
  specifically selected at checkout.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ORDER CUTOFF TIME
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Daily order cutoff: 2:00 PM Eastern Time (ET), Monday–Friday.

  • Orders placed BEFORE 2:00 PM ET on a business day ship THAT day.
  • Orders placed AFTER 2:00 PM ET, or on weekends/holidays, ship the
    NEXT business day.
  • Example: An Express order placed Friday at 3:00 PM ET ships Monday
    and arrives Wednesday (2 business days later).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CARRIER PARTNERS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Zeladija ships exclusively with the following carriers:
  • UPS (United Parcel Service) — primary carrier for Express and Overnight
  • USPS (United States Postal Service) — primary carrier for Standard to
    PO Boxes, APO/FPO, and rural routes
  • FedEx — used for select large-item and freight shipments

All tracking links are accessible at https://track.Zeladija.com using your
order ID or tracking number.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Delivery Time: 10–14 business days (may be longer due to customs clearance)
Cost: Calculated at checkout based on destination country and package weight.
    Typical rates range from $18.99 (Canada/Mexico) to $39.99+ (Europe/Asia).

Important International Notes:
  • Customers are responsible for all import duties, customs fees, and local
    taxes. Zeladija cannot estimate or prepay these charges.
  • Some products (e.g., high-wattage devices, radio-frequency devices) may
    be restricted in certain countries. The checkout process will flag
    restricted items.
  • International orders are not eligible for Overnight shipping.
  • International tracking may show gaps of 2–5 days during customs
    processing. This is normal and does not indicate a lost package.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ADDRESS & DELIVERY ISSUES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Ensure your shipping address is accurate before placing an order.
    Address changes after the cutoff time cannot be guaranteed.
  • If a package is returned to Zeladija due to an incorrect address or
    failed delivery, we will contact you to arrange re-shipment. A $5.99
    re-shipment fee applies.
  • For lost packages: If tracking shows "Delivered" but you have not
    received your order, please wait 24 hours (carriers sometimes pre-scan)
    and check with neighbors. If still missing, contact support within 7 days
    of the delivery date so we can open a carrier investigation.
