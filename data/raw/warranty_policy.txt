Zeladija Electronics — Product Warranty Policy
Last Updated: June 2026

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WARRANTY COVERAGE PERIODS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Zeladija provides a limited manufacturer's warranty on all products sold
directly through Zeladija.com and authorized Zeladija retailers.

  • Electronics (laptops, monitors, cameras, audio devices, smart home):
    1-Year Limited Warranty from date of purchase.

  • Accessories (cables, hubs, chargers, mounts, stands, wrist rests):
    90-Day Limited Warranty from date of purchase.

  • Consumables and filters: No warranty coverage.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT IS COVERED
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The Zeladija Limited Warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship
under normal use during the warranty period. Covered issues include:

  • Component failure not caused by external damage (e.g. screen backlight
    failure, battery not holding charge from day one, port stops working)
  • Manufacturing defects detected within the warranty period
  • Firmware issues introduced by official Zeladija firmware updates
  • Structural defects in the chassis or enclosure under normal handling

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT IS NOT COVERED
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The warranty does NOT cover:

  • Physical damage: cracks, dents, broken ports caused by drops or impacts
  • Liquid or water damage of any kind, even on IPX-rated devices if the
    damage is beyond the stated rating
  • Damage from use outside specified operating conditions (voltage surges,
    extreme temperatures, etc.)
  • Cosmetic damage: scratches, scuffs, discoloration from normal use
  • Damage from unauthorized modifications, non-official firmware, or
    third-party repairs
  • Products with removed, altered, or illegible serial number labels
  • Accessories sold separately (unless they are the claimed defective item)
  • Normal wear and tear (e.g. gradual battery capacity reduction over time)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO FILE A WARRANTY CLAIM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Step 1: Visit Zeladija.com/warranty or use the in-app support chat.
Step 2: Log in and select "File a Warranty Claim."
Step 3: Enter:
        • Order number (from your confirmation email)
        • Product serial number (found on the box or product label)
        • Clear description of the defect
        • Photos or video demonstrating the issue (required)
Step 4: Our warranty team will review your claim and respond via email
        within 1–2 business days.
Step 5: If approved, you will receive:
        • A prepaid return shipping label, and
        • A replacement unit or store credit, depending on stock availability.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WARRANTY CLAIM TURNAROUND TIME
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Claim review: 1–2 business days
  • Return shipping transit time: 2–5 business days
  • Inspection and processing: 2–3 business days
  • Replacement shipment: 1–3 business days after approval

Total estimated turnaround: 2–3 weeks from claim submission to receiving
a replacement. Rush processing is not available for standard warranty claims.
